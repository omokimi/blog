Today we are reviewing a game with a very unique premise: *In a World Full of Zombies, I'm the Only One Who Doesn't Get Attacked* (ゾンビのあふれた世界で俺だけが襲われない). 
今天我们来测评一款设定非常独特的游戏：《僵尸世界唯我独尊》（ゾンビのあふれた世界で俺だけが襲われない）。

## Story Setting | 剧情设定

![Apocalyptic City / 末日城市](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1470163395405-d2b80e7450ed?q=80&w=1200&auto=format&fit=crop)
*Image source: Unsplash (Used for Hexo rendering test / 仅用于 Hexo 渲染测试)*

While everyone else is struggling to survive the zombie apocalypse, the protagonist discovers a shocking truth: **Zombies completely ignore him!** 当所有人都在丧尸末日中艰难求生时，男主却发现了一个惊人的事实：**丧尸居然完全不咬他！**

Instead of a tense survival experience, the game turns into a relaxed scavenging simulator. You can walk right past the undead to loot supermarkets and secure resources.
这款游戏并没有紧张的求生体验，反而变成了一个轻松的“进货模拟器”。你可以大摇大摆地穿过尸群去超市零元购并囤积物资。

## Core Features | 核心亮点

* **Anti-Cliché Premise / 反套路设定:** You are not fighting for your life; you are basically managing a post-apocalyptic logistics network.
  你不是在为了生存而战斗，你基本上是在管理一个末世物流网络。
* **The Joy of Scavenging / 搜刮的乐趣:** Roaming around infected zones to collect items without any combat pressure is oddly satisfying and therapeutic.
  在感染区游荡收集物品，没有任何战斗压力，这种体验有一种奇怪的解压感。
* **Choices Matter / 剧情分支:** The survivors you meet and the supplies you choose to share will heavily influence the ending of the story.
  你遇到的幸存者以及你选择分享的物资，将很大程度上影响故事的结局。

## Review & Rating | 综合评分

> "In this collapsed world, I am the only one who is absolutely safe." 
> “在这个崩坏的世界里，只有我是绝对安全的。”

![Abandoned Supermarket / 废弃超市](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1582213782179-e0d53f98f2ca?q=80&w=1200&auto=format&fit=crop)

Overall, the pacing is very relaxing. The art style and CGs are of high quality, making it highly recommended for players who love apocalyptic themes but prefer a casual approach.
整体玩下来节奏非常轻松。美术风格和 CG 质量都非常高，强烈推荐给喜欢末日题材但又偏爱休闲玩法的玩家。
